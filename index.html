The Challenge

A Silent Crisis in Our Schools

In many rural government schools, adolescent girls face a silent struggle: the lack of safe and functional sanitation facilities.

At the Government High School in Vadakkampatti, 40 young girls hesitate to attend school due to unsafe, incomplete restroom structures. This isn't just about infrastructure — it affects their dignity, health, and causes many to miss school during menstruation, increasing the risk of dropping out.

No Safe Sanitation

Existing structures are incomplete, damaged, and unsafe for daily use

Attendance Drops Sharply

Girls regularly miss school days, especially during menstruation

High Dropout Risk

Without safe facilities, girls are pushed toward leaving school permanently
